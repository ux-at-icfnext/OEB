</span>How do I know what my after tax income is?</button>

<section class='ontario-accordion__content' id='accordion-content-8' aria-labelledby='accordion-button-id-8' aria-hidden='true' data-toggle='ontario-expander-content'>
<p>To find out what your after tax income is for the purposes of OESP eligibility, multiply your net income (line 236 on your Notice of Assessment) by 87.18%.</p>
<p>We encourage you to apply if your calculation of after tax income is close to the amounts shown below or if you are unsure of your after tax income.
<ul>
<li>For a 1 person household, the household income is $38,000 or less.</li>
<li>For a 2 person household, the household income is $54,000 or less.</li>
<li>For a 3 - 4 person household, the household income is $65,000 or less.</li>
<li>For a household of 5 or more people, the household income is $71,000 or less.</li>
</ul>
</p>

</span>How is my income, and the income of my household members, verified?</button>
</h3>
<section class='ontario-accordion__content' id='accordion-content-9' aria-labelledby='accordion-button-id-9' aria-hidden='true' data-toggle='ontario-expander-content'>
<p>The Ministry of Finance will verify your income with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) using your Social Insurance Number (SIN) and the SINs of any other income tax filers in your household. The CRA verifies your income as declared on your tax return from last year or the year before. If you or other members of your household filed taxes only once in the last two years, income verification can still occur. This is true even if you and another household member filed in different tax years.</p>
<p>If you have not filed an income tax return recently, or if your situation has changed since you last filed, your income will need to be manually verified by a designated intake agency. To view a map of intake agencies, click on <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/d/viewer?mid=1JHAgVrCuLAMYGWyhnYHTXc9vnOI">Google Maps.</a></p>
